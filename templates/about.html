Our Story

Founded in 2026, GovtExamWala started with a simple yet powerful mission: to help every aspirant crack government exams with confidence. What began as a small team of passionate educators has now grown into India's most trusted exam preparation platform.

We recognized that millions of students across India lacked access to quality, affordable, and structured preparation for competitive exams. The existing solutions were either too expensive, lacked personalization, or didn't provide real exam-like experience.

Today, GovtExamWala serves over 2 crore students, offering AI-powered personalized learning, real-time analytics, and comprehensive test series for all major government exams including SSC, Banking, Railway, UPSC, and more.
