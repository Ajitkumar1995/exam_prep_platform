General Questions

What is GovtExamWala?

GovtExamWala is India's leading online exam preparation platform that helps students prepare for various government competitive exams like SSC, Banking, Railway, UPSC, State PCS, Defence, and more. We offer mock tests, video lectures, study materials, current affairs, and AI-powered analytics.

Is GovtExamWala free to use?

GovtExamWala offers both free and paid content. We provide free mock tests, basic study materials, and practice questions. For advanced features like full-length tests, detailed analytics, and premium courses, we have affordable subscription plans starting at just ₹499/month.

Which exams does GovtExamWala cover?

We cover a wide range of government exams including:
  • Banking: SBI PO, SBI Clerk, IBPS PO, IBPS Clerk, RBI Grade B, RBI Assistant
  • SSC: SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, SSC MTS, SSC GD, SSC CPO
  • Railway: RRB NTPC, RRB JE, RRB Group D, RRB ALP
  • UPSC: Civil Services (IAS, IPS, IFS), CAPF, CDS
  • State PCS: UPPSC, BPSC, MPPSC, RPSC, etc.
  • Defence: NDA, CDS, AFCAT, CAPF
  • Insurance: LIC AAO, NIACL AO, UIIC AO
  • Teaching: CTET, UPTET, DSSSB, KVS, NVS

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple:
  1. Click on the "Register" button on the top right corner

  3. Verify your email with the OTP sent to your inbox

Exams & Preparation

How should I start my exam preparation?

We recommend the following approach:
  1. Understand the exam pattern and syllabus thoroughly
  2. Take a diagnostic mock test to assess your current level
  3. Follow the AI-generated personalized study plan
  4. Study topic-wise using our video lectures and notes
  5. Practice with topic-wise and sectional tests
  6. Attempt full-length mock tests regularly
  7. Analyze your performance and work on weak areas

How accurate are the mock tests compared to real exams?

Our mock tests are designed by experts who have analyzed previous years' papers and latest exam patterns. The difficulty level, question types, and interface closely match the actual exams. Many of our students have reported that the actual exam felt similar to our mock tests in terms of difficulty and pattern.

How does the AI-powered analytics work?

Our AI system analyzes your performance across multiple dimensions:
  • Tracks your accuracy and speed for each topic
  • Identifies your strengths and weak areas
  • Predicts your probable rank and percentile
  • Provides personalized recommendations for improvement
  • Compares your performance with toppers
  • Suggests targeted practice questions

Mock Tests

How many mock tests can I attempt?

Free users can attempt up to 5 mock tests per month. Premium subscribers get unlimited access to all mock tests, including full-length, sectional, and topic-wise tests. We have over 500+ mock tests covering all major exams.

Can I pause and resume a mock test?

Yes, you can pause a mock test and resume it later. However, the timer continues to run in the background. If you close the browser, your progress is saved, and you can resume from where you left off. Note that the total time limit remains the same.

Will I get solutions after the test?

Absolutely! After completing a mock test, you get:
  • Detailed solutions for every question
  • Video explanations for difficult questions
  • Shortcut tricks and alternative methods
  • Topic-wise performance analysis
  • Comparison with other test-takers

Is there negative marking in mock tests?

Yes, our mock tests follow the same marking scheme as the actual exam. If the real exam has negative marking, our mock tests will also have it. This helps you practice time management and avoid guesswork. The negative marking value is clearly mentioned before starting the test.

Technical Issues

What should I do if the test window freezes?

If the test window freezes, try the following:
  1. Refresh the page - your answers are auto-saved
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  4. Try using a different browser (Chrome is recommended)
  5. Contact our support team with screenshots

Is GovtExamWala available on mobile?

Yes! GovtExamWala is fully responsive and works on all devices. We also have dedicated mobile apps for Android and iOS available on Google Play Store and Apple App Store. You can access all features, including mock tests, video lectures, and study materials on your mobile phone.
